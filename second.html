About Kinesics:

Kinesics is a sign language convertor
Features:
1. A cost-effective solution to study in the dark without disturbing anybody.
2. Don't bother to look into the screen to read.
3. Sensitive and quick gesture control. Change page with a flick of your finger.
4. Suitable screen size for a better view of the book.
5. An Easy learning tutorial for kindergarten can easily be provided. Showing alphabets and numbers in a unique way.
6. While cooking one need not use their dirty hands to turn the pages of the recipe book, a simple hand gesture will do.
